Information Stored

Campfire Connections stores account, profile, enrollment, schedule, facility, faction, and reporting records needed to operate camp programs.

Access Scope

Navigation and data views are role-scoped. Faculty, leaders, attendees, and admins see records according to their facility, faction chain, or administrative role.

Operational Use

Stored records are used for registration, roster management, class scheduling, lodging coordination, and operational reporting.

Account Control

Users can review profile preferences from their account page. Administrators manage account access and role assignment for their organization.
